Why This Works

  • Every article gets a disposition — Keep, Update, Split, Merge, Archive, or Delete with clear reasoning
  • Prioritized by impact — High-traffic outdated content gets fixed first; low-traffic noise gets cleaned up after
  • Actionable, not just analytical — The audit IS the project plan — hand it to any writer and they can start executing
